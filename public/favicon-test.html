🛠️ Troubleshooting Steps

  1. Hard Refresh: Press Ctrl+F5 (PC) or Cmd+Shift+R (Mac)
  2. Clear Cache: Go to browser settings and clear cache/cookies for localhost
  3. Try Incognito: Open this page in incognito/private mode
  4. Wait: Sometimes favicons take 5-10 seconds to appear
  5. Check Console: Open DevTools and look for any favicon-related errors
