# Game Settings - Quick Reference

## How Settings Work

### Opening Settings
Each gamemode (Classic, Speedrun, Extra, Versus) has a **Settings button (⚙️)** in the top-right corner.
- Click to open the settings dialog
- Changes are applied immediately
- Settings persist across sessions

---

## Available Settings

### 🎮 Gameplay Settings

#### Auto-Repeat Movement
- **What it does**: Controls how movement keys behave when held
- **OFF**: Each key press moves the piece once (requires tapping)
- **ON** (default): Holding a key moves the piece continuously
- **Affects**: Arrow keys and WASD movement in all gamemodes

---

### 👀 Visual Settings

#### Show Ghost Piece
- **What it does**: Shows/hides the preview of where your piece will land
- **OFF**: Ghost piece hidden (cleaner view)
- **ON** (default): Ghost piece visible (helpful for planning)
- **Affects**: All gamemodes

#### Show Grid Lines
- **What it does**: Displays grid lines on the game board
- **OFF** (default): Clean board without grid
- **ON**: Visible grid helps with piece alignment
- **Affects**: All gamemodes

#### Particle Effects
- **What it does**: Controls animations when lines are cleared
- **OFF**: No line-clear animation (faster/cleaner)
- **ON** (default): Animated line-clear effects
- **Affects**: All gamemodes

---

## Audio Settings (Also Available)

- **Sound Effects**: Toggle on/off and adjust volume
- **Background Music**: Toggle on/off and adjust volume

---

## Default Settings

All settings can be reset to defaults using the **"Reset to Defaults"** button in the Settings dialog.

| Setting | Default |
|---------|---------|
| Auto-Repeat Movement | ON |
| Show Ghost Piece | ON |
| Show Grid Lines | OFF |
| Particle Effects | ON |
| Sound Effects | ON (70% volume) |
| Background Music | ON (50% volume) |

---

## Where Settings Apply

✅ **Classic Mode** - All settings functional  
✅ **Speedrun Mode** - All settings functional  
✅ **Extra Mode** - All settings functional  
✅ **Versus Mode** - All settings functional (both players)  

---

## Data Storage

Settings are saved to your browser's **Local Storage** and automatically restored when you return to the game.

Clear your browser data will reset all settings to defaults.
